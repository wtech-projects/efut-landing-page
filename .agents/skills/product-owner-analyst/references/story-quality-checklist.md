# Story Quality Checklist

Use this checklist before creating or updating the GitHub issue.

- Problem is explicit and tied to user/business value.
- Story statement (`As a / I want / So that`) is complete.
- Scope boundaries are clear (`In Scope` and `Out of Scope`).
- Functional requirements are specific and testable.
- Non-functional constraints are stated where needed.
- Architecture impact is mapped to real modules/components.
- API, data, and integration impacts are documented.
- Acceptance criteria are observable and verifiable.
- Dependencies and blockers are listed.
- Assumptions and open questions are separated from facts.
- Specialist feedback (backend/frontend) is incorporated.
- Work can be delivered incrementally (no oversized scope).
